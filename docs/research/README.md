## Why "research" and not "papers"?

Because DST is broader than academic papers.

This folder covers:
- 📊 **Macro analysis** — systemic stress at the market/org level
- 📐 **DST theory** — the formal model
- 🔬 **System analysis** — applied research across domains

> Papers are a subset of research. Research is the full picture.
